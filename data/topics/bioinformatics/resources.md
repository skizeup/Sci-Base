# Ressources — Bioinformatique

## Cours en ligne
- [MIT 7.91J — Foundations of Computational and Systems Biology](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/7-91j-foundations-of-computational-and-systems-biology-spring-2014/)
- [Coursera — Bioinformatics Specialization (UC San Diego)](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/bioinformatics)
- [Rosalind](https://rosalind.info/) — plateforme interactive de problèmes de bioinformatique
- [EMBL-EBI Training](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/training/) — formations en bioinformatique

## Livres
- *Bioinformatics: Sequence and Genome Analysis* — David W. Mount
- *Biological Sequence Analysis* — Durbin, Eddy, Krogh, Mitchison
- *An Introduction to Bioinformatics Algorithms* — Jones, Pevzner
- *Deep Learning for the Life Sciences* — Bharath Ramsundar et al.

## Outils
- [Biopython](https://biopython.org/) — bibliothèque Python pour la bioinformatique
- [BLAST](https://blast.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) — recherche de similarité de séquences
- [AlphaFold](https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk/) — prédiction de structure protéique
- [PyMOL](https://pymol.org/) — visualisation de structures moléculaires
- [Galaxy](https://usegalaxy.org/) — plateforme web d'analyse bioinformatique
- [RDKit](https://www.rdkit.org/) — cheminformatique en Python
